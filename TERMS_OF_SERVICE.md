## 5. Assumptions & Limitations

### Statutory References
Calculations based on:
- Employment Ordinance (Cap. 57)
- Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Ordinance (Cap. 485)
- Employment and Retirement Schemes Legislation (Offsetting Arrangement) (Amendment) Ordinance 2022
- HKICPA SME-FRS Section 28 & HKAS 19 / HKFRS for PE Section 28
- HKMA Exchange Fund Bills & Notes daily yields

### Key Limitations
1. **MPF Estimation**: Where actual balances are not provided, mandatory ER balance is estimated from hire date to valuation date using historical salary-cap segments (including post-1 May 2025 period when applicable). Always verify against trustee statements.
2. **Salary Projection**: Based on user-specified growth rate and assumes continued employment to retirement age (65).
3. **Discount Rate**: HKFRS only; assumes rates refreshed per HKAS 19 at each reporting date.
4. **Government Subsidy**: 25-year ladder subject to legislative change; verify applicable rates annually.
5. **Turnover Assumption**: SME-FRS assumes 100% retention; HKFRS applies probability only to post-transition burden.
6. **Retirement Age**: All employees assumed to retire at age 65; adjust turnover rate to reflect earlier departures.
7. **Five-Year Vesting**: Employees with <5 years service excluded; verify against contract terms.
8. **$390,000 Cap**: Total LSP capped regardless of service or salary; per Cap. 57.
